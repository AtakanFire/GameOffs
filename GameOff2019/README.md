## Gameplay

* The witch searching her books. Take all books and pass the level... 
* Keys:
  * Movement: W, A, S, D 
  * Create Step: Q or Shift
  * Take Book: E
  * Reset Steps: R
  * Find Book: 1
  * Next Level: F or Click Portal
  * Restart Level: P
